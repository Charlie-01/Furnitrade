Mission Statement

The primary mission of our business is providing college students an easy way to buy and sell used dorm furniture. Furnitrade also looks to be mindful of the sustainable development of our society. Trash in landfills significantly contributes to global warming by releasing methane gas, and each year, nine million tons of furniture end up in landfills. Our application seeks to not only provide the everyday person with the ease of buying and selling furniture, but also makes an impact in areas of consumerism and throwaway culture.
